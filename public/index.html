How It Works

  1. Send Files: As a sender, you select files for sharing. Our service generates a secret key.
  2. Share the Key: You securely share this key with your intended recipient.
  3. Receive Files: The recipient visits our site, enters their name and the secret key, and can then securely download the files.

Blinkdrop uses the official Google Drive API to manage files. Google Drive to store all the shared files to become accessible for the receiver.
