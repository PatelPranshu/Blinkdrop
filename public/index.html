Frequently Asked Questions

Is Blinkdrop free to use?

Yes, Blinkdrop is completely free for sharing files. We are supported by the ads on our content pages, which allows us to keep the service running without charging users.

How long are my files stored?

To protect your privacy and save space, all uploaded files and their associated transfer keys are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers after 24 hours.

What is the maximum file size?

You can upload files up to 10GB and a maximum of 100 files per transfer. This makes it easy to share high-resolution photos, videos, and large documents without any issues.

How does Blinkdrop keep my files secure?

We use end-to-end encryption. Your files are encrypted on your device using your unique key before being uploaded. This means only you and the recipient with the key can access the files. We, at Blinkdrop, cannot view your files.
